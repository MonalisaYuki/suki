Computer

Updated: 04/09/2024 by Computer Hope
A computer is a programmable device that stores, retrieves, and
processes data. The term "computer" was originally given to
humans (human computers) who performed numerical
calculations using mechanical calculators, such as theabacusand
slide rule. The term was later given to mechanical devices as they
began replacing human computers. Today's computers are
electronic devices that accept data (input), process that data,
produce output, and store (storage) the results (IPOS).
